The Five Principles

EFFORT

Putting forth an exertion of strength or power, physically, or mentally.

ETIQUETTE

The conventional code of good manners which governs behavior in society; professional and business decorum.

SINCERITY

Honesty of mind or intention; truthfulness. Always tell the truth. Say and do exactly what you mean.

SELF-CONTROL

Control over ones own impulses, emotions, and weaknesses. Always do what you have to do. Finish what you begin.

CHARACTER

The total sum of qualities making up an individual; moral excellence, the person that you really are.
